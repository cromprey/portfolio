Buffalo River Adventures

Final Project | ITEC-3610

Team: ACMD Development (Anthony Lin, Mya Souvanna, Caitlin Romprey, DaMarcus West)

Over the course of five weeks, my team and I helped our clients reach their goals of basic branding needs, create a website that will allow their customers to view their services and make reservations, and allow their customers to know more about the Buffalo River and if it is safe to canoe.

During this project, I was the main brand specialist. By helping create a name, logo, color palette and choosing fonts for their brand, this would help their audience be able to recognize Buffalo Outdoor Adventures and help them bring in new clients. We started this process by creating a user journey and wireframe, then a user-flow and prototype. This was able to allow our team to go in and hand-code a final website that fit our clients needs.
